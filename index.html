Introduction to Robotics & IoT

Step into the exciting world of Robotics and the Internet of Things (IoT), where hardware meets software to bring ideas to life.

  • Robotics combines mechanics, electronics, and programming to create intelligent machines that can sense, decide, and act. From simple moving bots to advanced industrial robots, the field covers automation, AI, and human-robot interaction.
  • IoT (Internet of Things) is about connecting everyday devices to the internet so they can collect, share, and act on data. From smart homes to connected cars, IoT makes systems more intelligent and interactive.
  • Arduino is a beginner-friendly microcontroller platform that helps you learn the basics of robotics and electronics. ESP32 is a more powerful board with built-in WiFi & Bluetooth, widely used in IoT projects. Together, they are the perfect starting point to explore Robotics and IoT hands-on.
